Do I have to have a Vimeo Profile?

Yes, every member has a Profile on Vimeo. We recommend that you customize it by uploading a portrait and maybe telling us a thing or two (or seven) about yourself. We have a very friendly community here, so we encourage you to enter our safe space and share your innermost secrets. Or maybe just your outermost secrets. We don't want things to get too real.

How do I edit my Profile?

You can get to your Profile Settings in a couple different ways. You can click Settings below the portrait on your Profile page, or hover your mouse over your profile image in the top navigation bar and click Account Settings. From there, choose the Profile tab.
